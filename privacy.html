Privacy Policy

At Layerbit, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy outlines our data practices for all visitors using our utilities.

Information Collection & Usage

We collect minimal personal information, such as email addresses, only when you contact us directly. We use industry-standard tools like Google Analytics and AdSense to collect non-personal data (IP address, browser type) to improve site performance and serve relevant advertisements.

Data Security

Because many of our tools are browser-based, sensitive technical data you input (like code, JSON payloads, or server logs) remains local to your device. For data we do collect, we implement robust security measures to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure.

Cookies & Advertising

Layerbit uses cookies to enhance user experience and serve personalized ads via Google AdSense. These third-party vendors may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website. You can opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.
