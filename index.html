Why Developers Choose Layerbit

The Ultimate Browser-Based Developer Toolkit

Layerbit is engineered to streamline modern development workflows by providing a comprehensive suite of secure, browser-based utilities. Whether you need to rapidly format complex SQL queries, validate nested JSON structures, or decode JSON Web Tokens (JWT) on the fly, our platform delivers instant results without the overhead of desktop installations.

Privacy First, Always Free

We understand that developers handle sensitive data. That is why tools like our Base64 Encoder, Regex Tester, and URL Encoder run locally in your browser. We do not track your input strings, store your code snippets, or monitor your workflow. Layerbit offers pure productivity, completely free of charge.

Collaborate with LayerLink

Beyond standard text utilities, Layerbit features LayerLink, an innovative browser-based screen sharing tool designed for remote IT support and pair programming. No software downloads are required—simply generate a secure link and start collaborating with your team or clients instantly.
