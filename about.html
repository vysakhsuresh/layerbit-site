About Layerbit

Layerbit is a developer-first platform dedicated to offering a curated suite of tools and services that make everyday tasks simpler, faster, and more efficient. Whether you're a coder, freelancer, tech enthusiast, or team leader, Layerbit brings high-quality browser-based utilities right to your fingertips — without the hassle of installations or signups.

From code formatting and automation to screen sharing and real-time collaboration, we’re building tools that save time, reduce friction, and increase productivity. Our mission is to empower individuals and teams with smart digital solutions that are easy to use, beautifully designed, and built for real-world challenges.

At Layerbit, we believe that great tools shouldn't be hidden behind paywalls or complexity. We’re constantly expanding our ecosystem to include utilities that matter most — driven by feedback, simplicity, and innovation.

Why Layerbit?

Because speed, clarity, and flexibility are no longer optional. Whether you're testing code, formatting data, or sharing your screen with a teammate, Layerbit is built to support your flow, not disrupt it.
