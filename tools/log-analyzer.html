Forensic Parsing Engine

Layerbit’s Log Analyzer doesn’t just colorize text—it acts as a client-side SIEM (Security Information and Event Management) tool. It uses complex Regex mapping to structure raw text into queryable data.

  • Syntax Extraction: Automatically isolates and badges severities (ERROR, FATAL, WARN, INFO, DEBUG) and grays out ISO/UTC timestamps to reduce visual noise.
  • Live Telemetry: Generates instant counts of failure rates. Click the metric badges to immediately isolate and view only specific severity levels.
  • Air-Gapped Security: Analyzing sensitive production logs? This tool processes strings strictly in your browser's DOM. No logs are ever uploaded to a server.
