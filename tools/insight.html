Device Fingerprinting Mechanics

Layerbit Device Insight utilizes native HTML5 APIs, WebGL rendering contexts, and IP routing databases to generate a comprehensive profile of your machine's footprint on the web. This data is what major platforms use to track, identify, or block you.

  • WebGL Extraction: Bypasses standard browser spoofing to query your physical GPU hardware directly.
  • Network Analysis: Estimates your connection type (e.g., 4G, Wifi) and downlink speed alongside your routing ISP.
  • Absolute Privacy: We do not log or store this telemetry. The data is pulled directly to your DOM for diagnostic awareness.
